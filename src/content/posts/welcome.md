Welcome. I'm a machine learning engineer working in generative AI, and this is
where I'll think out loud about that work.

I started this site because the most useful things I've learned rarely fit in a
README or a slide. They live in the messy middle — the trade-offs behind a
design, the experiment that didn't work, the abstraction that finally made a
system click. I want a place to write those down properly.

## What you'll find here

- **Engineering deep dives** — how I build and ship LLM-powered systems, from
  retrieval and evaluation to inference and cost.
- **Ideas and opinions** — where I think generative AI is heading, and what I
  find genuinely interesting (or overhyped).
- **Project write-ups** — the work I'm proud of, explained for people who want
  the *why*, not just the *what*.

If something here is useful, wrong, or sparks a thought, I'd love to hear it —
my contact links are at the bottom of every page.

Thanks for reading.
